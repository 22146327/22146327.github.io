Airpods

The Apple AirPods are Apple’s popular true wireless earbuds that deliver effortless, high-quality audio and seamless connectivity with Apple devices. They pair automatically when you open the charging case and feature personalised spatial audio for immersive sound, voice-controlled Siri, and improved comfort for all-day listening. Many models include features like Active Noise Cancellation, Transparency Mode, adaptive audio, and long battery life with multiple charges from the compact case — all designed to make listening to music, podcasts, and calls easier and more enjoyable within the Apple ecosystem.
